About Us

Welcome to MOUNIFULL, a home for authentic Lebanese mouneh – the traditional homemade foods passed down from one generation to another.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to connect the heart of Lebanese villages to every home across Lebanon. We help women market the delicious foods they prepare in their kitchens, made with love, care, and the finest natural ingredients.

Our Products

All our products are:

Natural & Pure

Handmade with Love

Traditional & Sustainable

Our Impact

By choosing our mouneh, you're not only enjoying authentic Lebanese flavors, but also supporting women's economic empowerment and helping preserve a beautiful culinary heritage.
